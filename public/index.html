Common Questions

Answers for
Informed Owners

Do you only work with legally permitted properties? +
Yes — exclusively. We only manage properties with valid STR permits or located in resort-zoned areas (Ko Olina, Waikiki, Turtle Bay). This protects you from fines that start at $1,000/day on Oahu.
What commission do you charge? +
22–28% of gross rental revenue depending on property and service scope. No hidden fees, no vendor markups. One transparent commission — that's it.
How do you price my property? +
We use PriceLabs dynamic pricing integrated with all your listings. Rates adjust daily based on local demand, competitor occupancy, and Hawaii seasonal patterns. Most properties see 20–25% more revenue vs. flat pricing.
Which platforms will my property be listed on? +
Airbnb, VRBO, and direct booking — all synced through a channel manager. No double bookings. We also build a direct booking channel over time to reduce your platform dependency and commission costs.
How quickly can we get started? +
Most properties are fully onboarded and listed within 14 days of signing. We have limited capacity — we are currently accepting new properties for Q2 2026. Reach out now to hold your spot.
